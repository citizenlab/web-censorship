The Adagmant Tommy not listening to Satish Seth


Satish Seth who was at his Adag (Amma daddy as gods) native place had to tame their adagmant Alsatian Tommy. He had a mind of his own or rather two. At the slightest opening of the gate he scooted out. It was when Satish Seth refused to oblige him he became mad at him. He just refused to come back to hom even after repeated calling him. This scene was also enjoyed by his friends 2G (2 gorilla sized) Surendra Hari, Scam Sam, Cbi (confused beyond intelligence) Gautaman and Swan alias Swana. So even dogs have their own minds about everything just like we humans do?
